ASH WALKER
Every road has a story. Every song is a mile.

Ash Walker is an AI-generated transmedia music project. Each chapter follows a war veteran riding west across America on his father's motorcycle — one song per mile, one mile per chapter.

This map traces his route from Tennessee to the Pacific. Click the green point to begin, or explore any marker.
